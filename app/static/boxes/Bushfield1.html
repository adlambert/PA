County School, like the infants school in Aylesbury St this building was designed by architects for Bucks County Council Messrs Ley Kirkham & Partners of Bishopsgate Without, London. Built in 1906 by E.Green of Northampton. Was the secondary school for Wolverton and North Bucks until the new building for Radcliffe School opened at the other end of the town, also known as Moon Street school, became Bushfield (middle) School in the 1970s when new main hall and a perpendicular classroom building were added.
